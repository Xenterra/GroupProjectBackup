Introduction

The purpose of the websites is to help users have access to live and historical data of air quality monitoring sensors in Aberdeen. The functionalities include:
- Access to various sensors location on a map
- Access to individual sensor details
- Access to live data, Past data and Data charts
- The ability to compare different sensors

Map

The page displays a map showing the location of each air monitoring sensor in Aberdeen. Click to access the map page.

Sensor List

The page shows a list of all air monitoring sensors with their details. Click to visit the sensor list page.

Comparisons

Compare and analyse historical data in data charts of different sensors. Click to go to the comparisons page.
